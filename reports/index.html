Insights and Compliance for Practice Excellent

Empower your practice with advanced reporting tools and compliance features designed to streamline operations and enhance patient care.

Reports & Compliance

  1. 2015 CURES Certified: Ensure compliance with advanced reporting features for Promoting Interoperability, Quality Measures, and UDS.

  2. MIPS and Quality Reporting: Generate QRDA I and III files for MIPS attestation for individual providers and track 28 certified quality measures across diverse specialties.

  3. Clinical Quality Alerts: Set up alerts for providers based on client conditions documented in the chart, using standards derived from Clinical Quality Measures.

  4. UDS Compliance: Stay updated with UDS reporting logic that is revised annually in accordance with the latest HRSN guidelines, ensuring practices can submit accurate numbers. UDS+ will be available to providers for the 2024 reporting year.

  5. Advanced Interoperability: Seamlessly exchange data across systems with CCDA and FHIR generation capabilities, ensuring smooth communication and compliance.

  1. Comprehensive Dashboard: Monitor weekly trends in visits, notes, charges generated, claims sent, rejections, denials, payments received, and A/R balances for a complete financial overview.

  2. Trend Analysis: Spot patterns and bottlenecks in your revenue cycle to address inefficiencies proactively.

  3. Optimized Financial Operations: Empower management with data-driven decisions to enhance revenue collection and streamline workflows.

  1. Real-Time Status Tracking: Monitor every visit’s lifecycle to ensure notes are signed off promptly for faster charge capture.

  2. Filter and Prioritize: Filter by provider, date of service, or note status to identify and address delays in documentation.

  3. Timely Note Completion: Highlight notes saved as drafts or completed but unsigned to reduce delays in billing and improve operational efficiency.

  4. Direct Provider Communication: Message providers directly from the dashboard to prompt timely note completion.

  5. Track Provider Productivity: Use provider-level filters to monitor the volume of visits providers are completing.

  6. Monitor Visit Attendance: Leverage appointment status filters to track attendance, cancellations, and no-shows for better operational oversight.

  1. End-to-End Visibility: Track each visit’s journey from visit completion to note sign-off, charge capture, claim creation, submission, and payment.

  2. Detailed Charge and Balance Insights: View charges associated with each visit along with the remaining balance, enabling clear financial tracking and follow-up actions.

  3. Payer and Claim Status Insights: Identify the next responsible party for payment, latest clearinghouse status, latest 835 status, whether the latest payment is paid or zero-paid, and aging of the claim.

  4. Customizable Filters: Filter data by date of service, charge status, claim status, and payer to prioritize actions.

  5. Detailed Event Tracking: Access granular details on each claim’s lifecycle to address bottlenecks and accelerate collections.

  6. Direct Provider or Staff Communication: Message providers or other staff members directly from the dashboard with questions or comments.

  7. For Billers and Practice Owners: Streamline claim management with detailed insights into claim status, payment progress, and next actions needed.

  2. Admissions Reports: Track patient enrollment and discharge statuses across programs to enhance care coordination, monitor progress, and optimize program capacity management.

  3. Bed Census Reports: Monitor real-time bed occupancy in inpatient facilities for efficient admission planning and resource management.

  4. Incoming Referrals Reports: Centralize and manage all referral activity to streamline patient onboarding and ensure no referral is missed.

  5. Outgoing Referrals Reports: Track the status of referrals sent to external providers or organizations, ensuring smooth care transitions and follow-ups.

  2. Preventive Care Reports: Identify patients due for preventive services like screenings and vaccinations.

  3. Execute Pending Orders Reports: Ensure timely completion of lab tests and imaging studies.

  4. eRx Reports: Monitor prescription statuses in real-time to ensure timely medication delivery.
