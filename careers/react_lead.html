Job Description

We are seeking an experienced ReactJS Developer with at least 6 years of software development experience and 4+ years of hands-on experience with ReactJS. The ideal candidate should be ready to lead a team of developers, contribute to technical decision-making, and mentor junior team members. This role is for someone who can balance technical expertise with strong leadership skills to help drive successful project outcomes.

Responsibilities:

  • ReactJS Development: Design and develop complex, high-performance applications with ReactJS and associated libraries, ensuring scalability and responsiveness.
  • Technical Leadership: Lead and mentor a team of developers, setting standards for code quality, best practices, and team collaboration.
  • Project Management: Oversee project timelines and delegate tasks, ensuring the development team meets deadlines and project goals.
  • Collaboration: Work closely with cross-functional teams, including UX/UI designers and back-end developers, to create cohesive and high-quality user experiences.
  • Architecture & Design: Design scalable and modular front-end architectures, implementing ReactJS best practices.
  • Code Review & Quality Assurance: Conduct regular code reviews, providing feedback to maintain high-quality, maintainable code.
  • Innovation: Keep up to date with the latest ReactJS trends and technologies, proactively suggesting improvements or alternative technologies where applicable.

Requirements:

  • Experience: At least 6 years of experience in software development with a minimum of 4 years of hands-on experience in ReactJS.
  • Skills: Strong proficiency in JavaScript, ES6, TypeScript, Redux, HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design principles.
  • Leadership Skills: Proven experience leading or mentoring a development team, with strong interpersonal and communication skills.
  • Technical Knowledge: Understanding of front-end build pipelines and tools (Webpack, Babel, etc.), RESTful APIs, and version control (Git).
  • Problem-Solving: Excellent problem-solving skills, with an analytical mindset and the ability to break down complex requirements.
  • Communication: Effective verbal and written communication skills to work collaboratively with internal teams and stakeholders.
  • Preferred: Exposure to cloud platforms like GCP, AWS, or Azure.
