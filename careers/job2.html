Senior ReactJS Developer – Individual Contributor

  • Exp 4-6 Years
  • Location Hyderabad(on-site)
  • Type Full-Time

We are looking for a skilled Senior ReactJS Developer with 4-6 years of experience in software development, including strong hands-on experience with ReactJS. This is an individual contributor role, ideal for someone passionate about building robust, scalable front-end applications. You will work closely with a cross-functional team to drive product development from concept to execution.

About Us

Adaptamed LLC is dedicated to creating cutting-edge healthcare technology solutions. As part of our team, you’ll be developing high-quality, user-friendly applications that empower healthcare professionals and streamline patient care. Join us in shaping the future of healthcare technology in a collaborative, growth-oriented environment.

Responsibilities

  • ReactJS Development: Develop complex, high-performance applications using ReactJS and associated libraries, ensuring scalability, responsiveness, and code efficiency.
  • Collaboration: Work alongside designers, backend developers, and product managers to implement features that deliver an outstanding user experience.
  • Architecture & Design: Contribute to front-end architecture, using best practices to build modular, reusable components and ensure maintainable code.
  • Code Quality: Write clean, high-quality, and well-documented code, following established coding standards and best practices.
  • Performance Optimization: Identify and resolve performance bottlenecks, ensuring applications are optimized for speed and scalability.
  • Testing: Implement unit and integration tests to ensure code quality and stability.
  • Learning & Innovation: Stay current with emerging front-end technologies and continuously improve your skill set, suggesting innovative approaches to improve the product.

Requirements

  • Experience: 4-6 years of experience in software development, with strong hands-on experience in ReactJS.
  • Skills: Strong proficiency in JavaScript, ES6, TypeScript, Redux, HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design principles.
  • Technical Knowledge: Familiarity with front-end build tools (Webpack, Babel, etc.), RESTful APIs, and version control (Git).
  • Problem-Solving: Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to work independently on complex issues.
  • Preferred: Exposure to cloud platforms like GCP, AWS, or Azure.
  • Communication: Excellent communication skills with the ability to work effectively in a collaborative environment and explain technical concepts clearly.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
  • Flexible working hours and a great remote work policy.
