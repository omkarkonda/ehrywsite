Inbox and Messaging

  1. Internal and External Messaging: Send messages to internal staff, the client/patient portal, or external email addresses for seamless communication.
  2. Pre-Defined Message Templates: Create and use standardized templates for consistent and efficient messaging.
  3. Document Attachments: Attach files directly from your computer or EHRYourWay for easy sharing.
  4. Client Portal Alerts: Send messages to the client/patient portal with email notifications to alert recipients of new messages.
  5. Bulk & Group Messaging: Send bulk messages to multiple clients at once or create user groups to send messages to multiple recipients at once.
  6. Automated Replies: Set up automated responses to streamline communication workflows.
  7. Message Visibility Permissions: Customize inbox visibility permissions for enhanced privacy and security.
  1. Draft Note Management: View and manage notes saved as drafts directly from your inbox for easy access and editing.
  2. Note Annotation: Annotate draft notes with comments to add context or clarification where needed.
  3. Review Completed Notes: Access and review completed notes forwarded by supervisees directly from your inbox for efficient oversight.
  4. Add Comments and Request Corrections: Add form-level or field-level comments and revert notes back to the supervisee for necessary corrections.
  5. Edit and Sign Off Notes: Edit, sign off, or bulk sign off draft or completed notes to streamline workflow and finalize documentation efficiently.
  6. Export and Share Options: Print, PDF, fax, or email draft notes for secure sharing and recordkeeping.
  1. Manage Registration Requests: View and manage client registration requests submitted through your practice’s website directly from your inbox.
  2. Accept or Decline Referrals: Accept or decline referrals with ease, triggering automated email notifications to clients with customizable email templates.
  3. Add Comments and Filter Records: Add comments to referral records and filter them by status (pending, approved, declined) for organized tracking.
  4. Automated Chart Creation: Automatically create charts for approved referrals, with demographic data seamlessly populated into the client’s chart.
  5. Export and Share Referral Data: Export referral information to Excel, and preview, print, or PDF referral details for documentation.
  6. Track Referral Actions: View a detailed log of actions performed on referral records to maintain accountability and transparency.
  1. Manage Appointment Requests: View and accept or decline appointment requests submitted via the client/patient portal directly from your inbox.
  2. Preview Demographic and Insurance Information: Access patient demographic and insurance details for informed decision-making.
  3. Schedule Accepted Appointments: Add accepted appointments directly to the provider’s schedule for a seamless workflow.
  4. Send Client Notifications: Send SMS messages to clients to confirm or communicate updates about their appointments.
  5. Filter Appointment Requests: Filter requests by status (Pending, Approved, Declined) for better organization and tracking.

View notes forwarded by other users

  1. View Completed Forms and Uploaded Documents: Access forms completed and documents uploaded by clients in the portal for review and action.
  2. Client Chart Access and Comments: Open the client’s chart directly from the portal and add comments or index documents to specific categories.
  3. Document Management Tools: Download, delete, or split documents as needed for efficient record organization.
  4. Permission Settings for Forms: Set permissions to control who can view forms and enable bulk sign-off for streamlined management.
  5. Export and Share Documents: Print, PDF, fax, or email forms and documents for secure and professional sharing.
  1. Informational (FYI) Note Access: View notes forwarded by other users for informational purposes.
  2. Export and Share FYI Notes: Print, PDF, fax, or email notes as required for secure distribution.
  3. Review Handed Over Notes: Access notes handed over by other supervisors who are on vacation for seamless task management.
  4. Customize Vacation Settings: Configure vacation details for supervisors to ensure proper note handovers and continuity.
  5. Client Chart Access and Note Editing: Open client charts to edit, sign off, or bulk sign off notes as needed.
  6. Add Comments to Notes: Annotate notes with comments to ensure clarity and effective handovers.
