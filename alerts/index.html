Stay Ahead with Intelligent Alerts and Automated Reminders

Streamline workflows and enhance compliance with powerful alert and reminder tools designed to keep your team informed and proactive.

Automated Reminders and Alerts

  1. Customizable Triggers: Set up automated reminders triggered by actions across the application, such as notes, documents, appointments, and authorizations.

  2. Centralized Management:View, manage, and monitor reminders in a single reminders window.

  3. Filter and Export: Filter reminders by date and export for tracking and reporting.

  4. Completion Tracking: Mark reminders as completed to maintain workflow efficiency

  5. Role-Based Access: Assign reminder access to specific users or roles for better delegation.

  6. Flexible Deactivation: Turn reminders off manually or automatically as conditions change.

  7. Documentation Blocking: Block note documentation based on reminder conditions, with automatic release when conditions are met.

  2. Multi-Point Access: View alerts from the Inbox, client chart, or Dashboard To-Do folder.

  3. Configurable Display: Choose where alerts appear, such as demographics, charts, or appointment schedules.

  4. Linked User Alerts: Target alerts to specific users or roles for precise notifications.

  5. Completion Permissions: Define who can mark alerts as completed – either the alert author or other users.
