Inbox and Messaging

Send messages to internal staff, to a client’s portal and to external emails

  1. Set up pre-defined message templates
  2. Attach documents from both your computer and EHRYourWay
  3. Send messages to the client/patient portal. Clients/patients will receive an alert notification to their personal emails to review the message in the portal.
  4. Create user groups to send bulk messages
  5. Send messages to external emails
  6. Create automated replies
  7. Send bulk messages to multiple clients at a time
  8. Set up visibility permissions for users’ inboxes

View your Saved as Draft notes and, directly from your Inbox

  1. Access the client/patient chart
  2. Edit and sign off notes
  3. Bulk sign-off draft notes
  4. Annotate your notes with comments
  5. Print/pdf/fax/email notes

Review notes that have been completed and forwarded to you by your supervises and, directly from your Inbox

  1. Access the client/patient chart directly
  2. Edit and sign off on notes
  3. Bulk sign off on notes
  4. Print/pdf/fax/email notes
  5. Add form-level or field-level comments to notes and revert the notes back to the Supervisee for corrections

View New Client Registration requests submitted from your practice’s website

  1. Accept or decline the referral directly from your Inbox
  2. Upon accepting or declining, the client will get an automated email notification.
  3. Create email templates for these email notifications
  4. Add comments to each referral record
  5. Filter referrals based on status (pending, approved, declined)
  6. Export referrals information to excel
  7. Preview the referral information entered by the new client and print/pdf it
  8. View a log of actions performed on referral records
  9. For approved referrals, automate chart creation for the client – Upon accepting the referral, demographic information provided in the new client
  10. registration form will automatically be loaded into the newly created chart
Color-code appointments based on:

Service, Appointment status, Encounter modality, Health plan and Location

View notes forwarded by other users

  1. Print/pdf/fax/email notes

View forms completed and documents uploaded by the client/patient in the client/patient portal

  1. Open the client’s chart
  2. Add comments to the documents
  3. Download documents
  4. Send a message to the client’s portal inbox
  5. Index documents to the required document category
  6. Delete and split documents
  7. Sign off on documents individually or in bulk
  8. Set permissions for who can see forms
  9. Bulk sign off on forms
  10. Print/pdf/fax/email forms

View and accept or decline appointment requests submitted by clients/patients from the client/patient portal

  1. Preview the patient’s demographic and insurance information
  2. Add accepted appointments directly to the provider’s schedule from your Inbox
  3. Send SMS messages to the client
  4. Filter appointment requests by status – Pending, Approved, Declined
  5. Open the client’s chart

Review notes handed over to you by other supervisors who are on vacation. Customize the vacations for the supervisors.

  1. Open the client’s chart
  2. Edit and sign off notes
  3. Bulk sign off notes
  4. Print/Pdf/Fax/Email notes
  5. Add comments to notes
