Streamline Your Appointment Scheduling with Ease

  1. Generate an appointment scheduler for each of your providers based on their working hours and availability.
  2. Specify appointment slots by visit type, encounter modality, and duration.
  3. Specify individual vs. recurring and one-on-one vs. group appointment slots and schedules.
  1. Schedule individual and recurring appointments.
  2. Schedule one-on-one and and group appointments.
  3. Give appointments by room and link the room to the provider to avoid double-booking a room
  4. Set the “bill to” party to ensure accurate and timely revenue collection
  5. Link the appointment to patient insurance details, including eligibility status and co-pay, for easy information access and co-pay collection later on
  6. Easily link the appointment to the co-pay amount for the CPT code of the service being provided in the appointment
  1. Schedule virtual appointments and have an automated Zoom, Google or Teams meeting link be linked to the appointment and sent to the client via email and SMS
  1. View for multiple providers at a time or an individual provider
  2. View by day, week, or month
  3. Filter by location, service, insurance, provider, and availability
