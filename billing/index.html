Boost Revenue, Reduce Errors, and Stay Ahead in Healthcare Billing

Effortlessly manage healthcare billing with automated claims processing, real-time payment tracking, error detection, and seamless integrations. Simplify workflows, enhance revenue cycle efficiency, and ensure compliance with secure, user-friendly EHR billing solutions."

Billing Pre CC4

Efficient Charge Capture

Our system streamlines charge capture by automatically collecting billing data from various sources, including appointment schedulers, group therapy sessions, and electronic notes. Transform your appointment scheduler into a powerful billing tool, where each check-in, check-out, or even missed or cancelled appointment triggers the capture of essential billing data. This feature ensures accuracy and ease, enhancing workflow efficiency and saving you time while reducing errors in your practice's billing information.

Billing for group therapy has never been easier. Our system is built to handle multiple patients simultaneously, ensuring that billing data for each session is accurately captured. Whether sessions are completed, missed, or cancelled, your billing is always up to date.

Our easy forms are designed with your workflow in mind. Providers can easily document services, and once completed, our system ensures that the billing is triggered. No manual entry needed — we automate the process to capture everything from service codes to provider information.

We simplify the billing process for inpatient and outpatient programs. Each day a patient is enrolled in a program, our system automatically captures a billing record. Whether it's a short outpatient session or an extended inpatient stay, all the necessary details are recorded effortlessly.

Managing paper documents? No problem. Our document management system allows you to upload and categorize all necessary paperwork. Billable documents, such as court service records, can be easily flagged to ensure no billing opportunity is missed. Non-billable documents are stored for easy access without cluttering your billing data.

Every practice is unique, and our system reflects that. We offer special billing rules that adapt to your specific workflow. From auto-capturing diagnosis codes to adjusting charges based on service durations, our system is built to handle complex billing scenarios seamlessly.

Streamline your authorization process with our system's automatic capturing of authorization details directly into claims. Units are deducted based on the documented authorization, ensuring compliance and keeping your billing accurate and up to date.

Customize your fee schedule rules to meet your practice's unique needs, enabling the automatic application of charges based on parameters like service type, provider, duration, and location. This flexibility minimizes manual adjustments and ensures accurate billing every time.

Achieve complete visibility into your billing process with our monitoring reports, allowing you to track the status of records—whether signed off, saved as drafts, or completed. Gain valuable insights to enhance practice management and optimize revenue cycle performance.

Billing & Online Payments
(everything Post-CC4)

Comprehensive Claims Billing and Payment Management Solution

Our advanced billing system is designed to streamline and simplify the entire claims and payment process for healthcare providers. Supporting industry-standard claim formats, extensive customization options, and seamless payment integration, our solution ensures efficiency, accuracy, and compliance at every stage.

Claims Billing Formats

We offer support for various standard billing formats to accommodate a wide range of services:
  • CMS1500 for outpatient services
  • UB04 for inpatient services
  • Customized Invoice/Superbill for third-party payers
  • Claim Form Customizations
Our system provides extensive customization options for claims, allowing you to tailor forms to meet the specific needs of your practice or payer
  • Supervisor NPI and Taxonomy in Box 24J of CMS1500
  • Organization NPI in Box 24J
  • Organization Details in Box 33, customizable by Program, Payer, and Location
  • Facility NPI in Box 32A for service location identification
  • NDC Codes for Drug CPT billing
  • Claim Numbers for laboratory services based on the facility

Claims Sending Formats

Efficiently submit claims electronically or by mail, depending on your operational needs:
  • 837P Format for CMS1500 claims
  • 837I Format for UB04 claims
  • Printed Claims for CMS1500 and UB04 for manual mailing
  • Customized Invoices for third-party billing
  • Patient Statement Customization

We can customize patient statements to fit your practice’s needs. Statements are easily accessible via the patient portal, allowing patients to view and settle outstanding balances effortlessly.

Rejection and Denial Management
  • Our system includes dedicated modules to handle claim rejections and denials, minimizing delays and boosting revenue:
  • Rejection Management: Identify, correct, and resubmit rejected claims quickly and accurately.
  • Denial Management: Track denials and their reasons, enabling efficient correction and resubmission from a single interface.

Payments Posting - Simplify payment collection with integrated tools for both automated and manual payment posting:

  • Credit Card Integration: Accept payments through Nexio, including credit card, bank account (ACH), and POS terminal transactions.
  • Electronic Remittance Advice (ERA): Automate payment posting by receiving detailed payment information directly from the clearinghouse.
  • Import ERA: Import ERA files manually, even from payer portals, for flexible payment management.
  • Manual Payment Posting: Post patient payments received via cash, checks, PayPal, or other sources manually.
  • Insurance Paper EOB Posting: Manually enter Explanation of Benefits (EOB) for paper-based claims.
  • Refunds and Void Transactions
  • Process refunds or void credit card payments efficiently through the Nexio gateway. Document manual refunds using various methods, including cash and checks, for complete transparency.
  • Payment Application
  • Our system offers flexible payment application features to ensure accurate allocation of funds
  • Manual Payment Application: Apply insurance or patient payments manually to individual claims.
  • Bulk Payment Application: Utilize the VLV (Volume Large Value) feature to efficiently apply multiple payments in one go.
  • Charges and Payment Locking
  • Maintain the integrity of your financial data by securing important actions within the system:
  • Lock the creation, modification, or deletion of charges.
  • Secure payment-related actions such as posting, applying payments, modifying applied payments, and deleting payments.
  • Write-Off Charges
  • Easily write off outstanding balances for individual claims or manage batch write-offs for multiple claims, improving financial efficiency.
  • Copay Charges
  • Seamlessly manage co-pays with automated features that create copay bills. Collect and apply copay payments upfront during patient check-in, reducing administrative burden.
  • Integrated In-House Clearinghouse
  • Our system includes an integrated clearinghouse (Claim.MD) that automates key aspects of the claims submission and payment process:
  • Real-time Insurance Eligibility Checks: Verify patient eligibility for services before claims are submitted.
  • Electronic Claim Submission and Status Updates (277): Submit claims electronically and track their status with ease.
  • Electronic Payment Receipt (ERAs): Automatically receive payment information from insurers and apply it to your system.
  • Automation Services
  • Maximize efficiency with comprehensive automation options for claims and payment management
  • Claims Creation and Submission: Automatically generate and submit insurance claims.
  • Patient Statements: Create and submit patient statements to the portal automatically.
  • Automatic Payment Collection: Set up scheduled payment plans (monthly, weekly, or one-time) for credit cards or bank accounts (ACH).
  • Automatic Payment Receipts: Automatically send receipts to patients after payments are collected.
  • Automated Payment Application: Automatically apply patient payments to outstanding balances, including co-pays.
  • Billing Reports
  • Gain valuable insights into your billing and revenue cycle with customizable reports
  • Accounts Receivable: Track outstanding claims and boost cash flow by focusing on unpaid balances.
  • Charges Report: Analyze charges (insurance and self-pay) based on the Date of Service (DOS).
  • Collection Reports: Track payments by posted date, applied date, or DOS for a clear view of your financial health.
  • Sent Claims Tracking: Monitor the status of electronically submitted claims through the clearinghouse.
  • Payroll Reports: Generate dynamic payroll reports based on either a percentage of applied payments or a flat rate per session, providing greater transparency for staff compensation.
