Boost Revenue, Reduce Errors, and Stay Ahead in Healthcare Billing

Effortlessly manage healthcare billing with automated claims processing, real-time payment tracking, error detection, and seamless integrations. Simplify workflows, enhance revenue cycle efficiency, and ensure compliance with secure, user-friendly EHR billing solutions."

Visit / Note

Automated and On-Demand
Eligibility Checks 

  • On-Demand Checks: Run eligibility checks in real-time from client charts, appointments, or billing windows. 
  • Automated Checks Scheduling: Set eligibility checks to run daily, weekly, monthly, or annually, on future appointments or across client population. 
  • Multiple Check Runs: Set multiple automated eligibility check runs by health plan, enabling precise and timely verification. 
  • Centralized Review Window: Access all completed eligibility checks with clear statuses (active, inactive, failed) in one window. 
  • Payor Identification: Identify additional payors like Medicare Advantage or HMO plans for accurate billing. 
  • Service-Specific Checks: Configure eligibility checks for specific services, such as Mental Health, to surface relevant results. 
  • Customizable Billing Fields: Display specific billing-related fields in the appointments window and limit access to authorized users or roles for enhanced control.
  • Comprehensive Billing Details: View copays, eligibility, and insurance information within the appointment for efficient copay collection and updates during the visit.
  • Billing Party Linkage: Automatically link appointments to the "bill to" party (insurance or client, if client is specified to be self-pay).
  • Copay Linkage: Automatically link appointments to co-pay amounts for streamlined collections.
  • Service-Specific Copay Integration: Link appointments to the copay amount for the CPT code of the service documented in the appointment.
  • Health Plan End Date Alerts: Get notified real-time if a health plan end date conflicts with future appointments, with options provided right then to reassign the billed party for those future appointments.

Appointment-Level
Billing Integration

Charge Capture and Claim Submission

Efficient Charge Capture and
Billing Automation

  • Customizable Billing Triggers: Configure charge capture triggers based on workflow needs, such as check-in, check-out, missed or cancelled appointments, or note sign-off and co-sign.
  • Appointment-Based Charge Capture: Include essential billing details directly in appointment fields for automatic inclusion in claims during creation.
  • Note Billing Auto-Population: Automatically populate notes with billing data from appointments, capturing service codes, provider details, and other information for accurate claim creation.
  • Group Therapy Billing: Automatically capture billing data for group sessions, whether completed, missed, or cancelled, eliminating manual adjustments.
  • Admissions Billing: Generate daily billing records for clients enrolled in inpatient or outpatient programs, ensuring accurate charge capture for both short-term and extended stays.
  • Authorization Auto-Capturing: Automatically capture authorization details into claims and deduct units based on the documented authorizations for compliance.
  • Centralized Charge Review: Access all charges ready for claims in one window to review and ensure accuracy.
  • Flexible Claim Submission: Automate claim creation and submission or manually create and submit, individually or in batches.
  • Comprehensive Billing Formats: Support multiple formats, including CMS1500 for outpatient services, UB04 for inpatient services, and customized invoices or superbills for third-party payers.
  • Customizable Claim Forms: Set up rules to auto-populate key fields like Box 24J (NPI and Taxonomy), Box 33 (Organization Details), Box 32A (Facility NPI), NDC codes for drug CPT billing, and CLIA numbers for laboratory services.
  • Automated Format Conversion: Automatically convert CMS1500 claims to UB04 format for streamlined submission of inpatient or institutional billing.
  • Multiple Submission Methods: Submit claims electronically (837P for CMS1500, 837I for UB04) or by mail using printed claims.
  • Batch or Single Submission: Create and send one or multiple claims at once with a single click, improving efficiency.
  • Client Statement Generation: Automatically generate and send client statements for self-pay clients via the portal or email based on their preference.
  • Customizable Statements and Invoices: Tailor client statements and third-party invoices to meet practice needs, with easy access through the client portal.
  • Unapplied Payment Allocation: Apply collected copay payments to outstanding client balances for accurate invoicing.

Claim Resolution

Automated or Manual
Payment Application

  • Electronic Remittance Advice (ERA): Automatically receive ERAs from clearinghouses or manually import ERA files from payer portals for flexible payment processing.
  • Zero-Paid vs. Paid Check Segregation: The software separates zero-payment checks from those with payments, allowing billers to quickly apply payments individually or in bulk for non-zero checks while focusing on manual review for zero-payment checks in a dedicated window.
  • Actionable Zero-Paid Check Handling: Review adjustment code reasons for zero-payment checks and take actions like claim resubmission, balance transfer to the client, or writing off balances directly from the zero-paid window.
  • Manual Payment Posting: Enter insurance or client payments manually for cash, checks, PayPal, or other sources.
  • Paper EOB Entry: Record Explanation of Benefits (EOB) details from paper-based claims manually to ensure accurate financial records.

Rejection and Denial Management

  • Reason Visibility: View rejection and denial reasons in centralized windows for targeted action.
  • Actionable Filters: Filter by rejection or denial reason or by payer to address rejections and denials based on specific reason or payer-specific workflows and guidelines.
  • Streamlined Workflow: Edit, resubmit claims, transfer balances, or write off directly from these windows.

Comprehensive Claims Management Window

  • Track Balances and Status: Monitor claim balances, payer details (primary, secondary, tertiary, or client), and claim aging in one view.
  • Next Payer Transition: Transition claims to the next payer or generate client statements directly from the window.
  • Credit Card Integration: Accept client payments via Nexio, supporting credit card, ACH, and POS terminal transactions for convenience.
  • Automatic Payment Collection: Set up scheduled payment plans (monthly, bi-weekly, weekly, or one-time) for client balances, ensuring timely payments.
  • Automatic Payment Receipts: Automatically send receipts to clients after payments are collected, improving communication and transparency.
  • Copay Charges: Automate the creation of copay bills and collect payments upfront during check-in, minimizing administrative burden.

Client Payment Features

Flexible Refund Management

  • Quick Refunds: Process refunds or void credit card payments efficiently through Nexio.
  • Manual Refund Documentation: Track refunds processed via cash or check for audit readiness.

Write-Off and Financial Controls

  • Batch Write-Offs: Manage financial adjustments efficiently with individual or batch write-offs.
  • Charge Locking: Secure the creation, modification, or deletion of charges to maintain data integrity.

Integrated In-House Clearinghouse

  • Real-Time Eligibility: Verify insurance before submitting claims to reduce denials.
  • Electronic Submissions: Submit claims electronically with real-time status tracking.
  • Payment Reconciliation: Automatically reconcile insurer payments via ERAs.

Billing Administrative Tools

  • Health Plan Management: Document and organize health plans by type (Medicare, Medicaid, Commercial) in a dedicated window.
  • CPT Code Management: Document and link CPT codes to services for accurate billing.
  • Billing Trigger Configuration: Set triggers to link programs and services to forms and billing events.
  • Fee Schedule Rules: Define fee rules based on service type, provider, duration, and location, with automated charge capture of the correct fee based on the parameters of the visit.
  • Custom Billing Rules: Set tailored rules to auto-capture diagnosis codes or adjust charges based on service duration or other parameters.
