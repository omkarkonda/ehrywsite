Revolutionize Client Onboarding with Automated Registration

Streamline paperwork, improve data accuracy, and deliver a seamless new client registration experience through a fully customizable and mobile-friendly solution tailored to your practice’s unique needs.

New Client Registration

  1. Tailored Data Collection: Customize registration forms to gather the exact information your practice requires.

  2. Workflow Alignment: Add or modify fields and modules to match your organization's workflows.

  3. Professional, User-Friendly Design: Create intuitive forms with a clean layout for easy use and complete data capture.

  1. Demographic Information: Capture essential client details like name, date of birth, and address.

  2. Policy Acknowledgment: Include sections for clients to agree to your practice’s terms and policies.

  3. Emergency Contacts: Collect emergency contact details for added safety and care.

  4. Referral Tracking: Record how clients discovered your practice for better insights.

  5. Document Uploads: Enable clients to upload key documents, such as insurance cards, directly into the form.

  1. Information Routing: Automatically send submitted registration forms to staff inboxes in EHRYW for review.

  2. Registration Approval: Accept or reject registrations based on your practice's criteria for controlled onboarding.

  3. Custom Workflow Automation: Configure rules to streamline onboarding, such as auto-admitting clients upon acceptance.

  4. Automated Chart Creation: Automatically generate client charts pre-filled with registration data, reducing manual entry.

  5. Direct Appointment Scheduling: Schedule the client’s first appointment directly from the inbox for a seamless transition to care.

  6. Automated Notifications: Notify clients via pre-templated emails about their registration status and next steps.

  1. Responsive Compatibility: Forms are optimized for smartphones and tablets with touch-friendly fields and fonts.

  2. Seamless Multi-Device Experience: Ensure a consistent, user-friendly interface across all devices.

  1. Prominent Logo Placement: Feature your practice’s logo on the registration webpage for strong branding.

  2. Customizable Visual Design: Match the page’s background imagery or color scheme to your website.

  3. Branded URL Integration: Use a unique URL to integrate the registration page into your website while maintaining brand consistency.

  4. Accessible Contact Information: Display your practice’s contact details prominently for client convenience.

  1. Client-Friendly Process: Offer a professional, intuitive registration experience to boost client satisfaction.

  2. Efficient, Accurate Onboarding: Reduce front-office workload, improve data accuracy, and streamline onboarding with an automated system.
