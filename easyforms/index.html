  5. Auto-Save: Prevent data loss with configurable auto-save intervals (15-60 seconds) and recovery options.
  6. Mobile-Friendly Compatibility: Ensure seamless documentation and accessibility across all mobile devices for enhanced flexibility and convenience.

  3. Void and Un void Signed Forms: Manage invalid forms with the ability to void or reinstate them.
  4. Unsign Notes: Unlock signed-off forms for edits as necessary, while maintaining an audit log of such actions.
  5. Field and Form-Level Comments: Add contextual notes at both the field and form levels for clarity or to revert the form back to the original documenter for revisions.
