Digitize Your Existing Forms Without Changing Your Workflow

Seamlessly transition to a paperless system with digital forms built from your existing templates. Preserve your practice’s workflows, minimize disruption, and avoid retraining staff by letting us digitize your familiar forms for effortless documentation and form-forwarding.

Easy Form Features

  1. Side-by-Side Notes: Reference previously saved notes alongside current ones without switching tabs, ensuring seamless documentation.

  2. HPI Tree View: Customize a hierarchical structure for HPI documentation with intuitive keywords and branching logic that auto-generate complete, structured sentences.

  3. Smart Phrases: Use customizable text shortcuts to quickly insert prewritten phrases or templates, saving time and ensuring accuracy.

  4. Shortcut Keys: Customize navigation shortcuts with Ctrl, Alt, or Shift for faster access.

  5. Auto-Save: Prevent data loss with configurable auto-save intervals (15-60 seconds) and recovery options.

  6. Mobile-Friendly Compatibility: Access and document seamlessly on all mobile devices for flexibility and convenience.

  1. Form Lifecycle Management: Save drafts, mark forms as completed and ready for supervisor review, sign off, or co-sign forms with ease.

  2. Addendums and Amendments: Append notes to signed forms while maintaining the integrity of original documentation.

  3. Void and Reinstate Forms: Void or reinstate signed forms as needed.

  4. Editable Signed Notes: Unsign notes for necessary edits while maintaining an audit log.

  5. Contextual Comments: Add comments at field or form levels to clarify or request revisions.

  6. Document Attachments: Index and attach files to a client’s chart within saved forms.

  7. Electronic Signatures: Include digital signatures with the user’s name, date, and time for verification.

  1. Auto Population: Automatically fill form fields with data from demographics, admissions, appointments, or other sources.

  2. Inter-Form Data Transfer: Enable field-level auto-population across forms to reduce repetitive data entry.

  1. Treatment Plan Templates: Use predefined templates to ensure consistency and continuity of care.

  2. Customizable Master Lists: Personalize Goals, Objectives, and Interventions to align with your practice's requirements.

  1. Dragon Integration: Enable voice command functionality for hands-free form completion and increased efficiency.

  2. Nabla Integration: Use AI-powered documentation support to minimize manual data entry and enhance accuracy.

  1. Comprehensive Form Forwarding: Direct forms to users or supervisors for review, co-signatures, comments, or as references.

  2. Manual and Automated Forwarding: Forward forms manually or via a rules engine, with user-level rules for automated routing.

  3. Supervisor Feedback: Supervisors can review forms, add comments, and return them for revisions to ensure accuracy.

  1. Patient Portal Integration: Upload drafts or completed forms for patients to review, complete, and sign electronically.

  2. Survey Forms: Send URLs to clients for survey documentation through email or the portal.

  1. Custom Permissions: Set granular controls for form access, editing, and documentation based on user roles.

  2. Audit-Friendly Notes: Track all actions and changes to forms to ensure integrity throughout the lifecycle.
