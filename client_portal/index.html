Client Portal

Relieve the load on your front-desk staff by allowing your clients to self-schedule or request new appointments directly from the portal, as well view their previously scheduled and future appointments.

Avoid Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet telehealth appointment links getting lost in emails and SMS by enabling your clients to securely access their telehealth appointment directly through the portal.

Secure messaging between the client and clinic is available through the portal, enhancing direct communication.

Allow clients to view and settle any outstanding balances directly via the portal by using credit or debit cards. Provide payment receipts for clients to access and download.

Send consent forms and intake forms requiring signatures or documentation to clients through the portal. Clients can sign and submit these forms electronically back to the clinic.

Clients can upload relevant documents or files for submission to your practice through the portal.

Clients can access clinical notes, lab reports, imaging results, and other documents shared by the clinic for review.

Allow our clients to self-maintain their personal information with the ability to review, update, and add a variety of personal information into the portal. All additions and updates will automatically be reflected in EHRYourWay. This includes demographic information, insurance policies, and emergency contact details.

Clients can view their current medications, submit prescription refill requests, and add any new medications to their profile.
