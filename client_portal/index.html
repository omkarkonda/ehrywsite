Empower Client Engagement with Seamless and Secure Access

Transform how your practice connects with clients through our versatile and secure client portal. Designed to align with your workflows, the portal streamlines engagement by offering a range of functionalities, including the ability for clients to request or self-schedule appointments, securely message providers and staff, view and pay bills, and much more—all through one intuitive interface.

Client Portal

  1. Lower front-office burden by allowing clients to book appointments directly through the portal, fully integrated with your providers’ schedules, or request appointments if your workflow requires.
  2. Clients can easily book or request appointments by selecting the desired service, modality, facility, and provider, which generates a filtered list of available days and times for seamless selection.
  3. Key appointment details, such as date, time, location or telehealth link, provider, program, and service, are prominently displayed on the portal homepage for easy client access.
  4. Clients can view both their upcoming appointments and their appointment history with complete details for better transparency and organization.
  5. Simplify telehealth workflows by displaying virtual appointment links directly on the portal, eliminating the need to send individual Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet links.
  6. Clients can securely access their telehealth links from the portal homepage for quick and convenient virtual appointment access.
  1. Streamline communication between clients, providers, and staff with secure messaging directly through the portal.
  2. Most recent messages, with unread messages clearly highlighted in bold, are easily accessible on the client’s portal homepage.
  3. Clients can send new messages directly from the portal homepage, minimizing clicks and enhancing convenience.
  4. The messaging interface includes inbox, sent, and trash folders to help clients efficiently organize their messages.
  5. Clients can multi-select and delete messages to maintain a tidy and manageable inbox.
  1. Client balances due are prominently displayed on the homepage, making it easy for clients to see and resolve outstanding amounts.
  2. Buttons to pay bills and view statements are readily available on the homepage, enabling clients to quickly review and pay amounts owed, accelerating patient balance collection for your practice.
  3. Clients can make payments via credit card or ACH, with the option to securely save payment information for faster transactions in the future.
  4. Clients can designate a default payment method, whether a credit card or bank account, for added convenience.
  5. Clients can opt to set up auto-pay, ensuring timely and consistent collection of patient balances.
  1. Collect and manage patient forms digitally to reduce overhead and streamline documentation workflows.
  2. Upload any variety or number of forms to the portal, including intake forms, credit card authorizations, questionnaires, surveys, and consent forms.
  3. Choose between manually uploading forms or enabling automated uploads to the portal for forms you need clients to complete.
  4. Set up automated form uploads based on specific trigger events, such as the creation of a new client chart, a designated date, or a recurring frequency.
  5. Enable clients to securely sign and submit forms through the portal with built-in e-signature capabilities.
  1. Enable secure two-way document sharing between clients and your practice through the portal.
  2. Clients and practices can upload and store a variety of documents for easy sharing and access, including insurance card images, medical records, visit notes, and other essential documents.
  1. Clients can document all their medications within the portal, giving providers full visibility for more informed care.
  2. Clients can request prescription refills through the portal, avoiding time-consuming phone calls and increasing the efficiency and speed of prescription refill processing by your staff.
  1. Insurance details are prominently displayed on the homepage, synced with the client's insurance information saved in EHRYW, and editable by the client – The high visibility encourages regular review and updating as needed to minimize billing disruptions.
  2. Clients can update their personal details within the portal, with changes seamlessly linked to their chart in EHRYW for accurate record maintenance.
  3. Clients can save and manage their preferred office location, pharmacy, lab facility, and x-ray facility for added convenience and customization.
  1. Clients can view provider-placed orders for labs or imaging directly within the portal.
  2. Lab and imaging results are available in the portal for clients to access and review, ensuring transparency and minimizing delays in sharing critical information.
