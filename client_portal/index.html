Empower Client Engagement with Seamless and Secure Access

Transform how your practice connects with clients through our versatile and secure client portal. Designed to align with your workflows, the portal streamlines engagement by offering a range of functionalities, including the ability for clients to request or self-schedule appointments, securely message providers and staff, view and pay bills, and much more—all through one intuitive interface.

Client Portal

  1. Client-Driven Booking: Clients can book or request appointments directly through the portal, integrated with provider schedules, reducing front-office workload.

  2. Seamless Scheduling: Clients can select a service, modality, facility, and provider to generate a filtered list of available days and times for easy booking.

  3. Prominent Appointment Details: Display key details, including date, time, location, telehealth link, provider, program, and service, on the portal homepage for quick access.

  4. Appointment History: Clients can view both upcoming and past appointments.

  5. Simplified Telehealth Access: Provide virtual appointment links directly on the portal homepage, eliminating the need for separate email or text communications.

  1. Secure Communication: Streamline client, provider, and staff messaging via a secure portal interface.

  2. Unread Message Highlights: Recent unread messages are bolded and displayed prominently on the homepage.

  3. Convenient Messaging: Clients can send new messages directly from the homepage with minimal clicks.

  4. Organized Messaging Interface: Inbox, sent, and trash folders allow for efficient message organization.

  5. Inbox Management: Clients can multi-select and delete messages to maintain an organized inbox.

  6. Threaded Conversation View: Back-and-forth messaging is displayed in a continuous thread for easy context and continuity.

  1. Transparent Balances: Client balances due are prominently displayed on the homepage to encourage timely payments.

  2. Quick Payment Options: Buttons for paying bills and viewing statements are readily available on the homepage for convenience.

  3. Flexible Payment Methods: Clients can pay via credit card or ACH, with secure storage of payment information for future use.

  4. Default Payment Method: Clients can designate a preferred payment method for added ease and efficiency.

  5. Auto-Pay Enrollment: Enable clients to opt into auto-pay for consistent, timely balance collection.

  1. Digital Form Management: Streamline documentation by collecting and managing client forms digitally.

  2. Flexible Uploads: Upload various forms, including intake forms, credit card authorizations, questionnaires, and consent forms.

  3. Automated Uploads: Automatically upload forms based on trigger events, such as new client chart creation or recurring dates.

  4. E-Signature Integration: Allow clients to securely sign and submit forms electronically through the portal.

  1. Secure Sharing: Facilitate two-way document sharing between clients and your practice.

  2. Versatile Document Management: Clients and practices can upload and store essential documents such as insurance cards, medical records, visit notes, and more for secure access.

  1. Medication Documentation: Clients can document their medications within the portal, giving providers full visibility for more informed care.

  2. Streamlined Refill Requests: Clients can request prescription refills through the portal, reducing phone calls and speeding up processing.

  1. Insurance Management: Clients can edit insurance details within the portal, with updates syncing to the client’s EHRYW chart.

  2. Personal Information Updates: Clients can update their personal details, seamlessly linking changes to their EHRYW chart for accurate records.

  3. Preference Management: Clients can save preferred office locations, pharmacies, labs, and imaging facilities for convenience.

  1. Lab and Imaging Orders: Clients can view provider-placed lab or imaging orders directly through the portal.

  2. Results Access: Provide lab and imaging results for clients to review in the portal, ensuring timely and transparent information sharing.
