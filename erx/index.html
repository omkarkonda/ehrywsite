eRx AND PDMP

eLabs

We are electronically connected with over 40 labs, including LabCorp and Quest, through a bi-directional interface. Our system provides alerts upon receiving lab results and allows providers to review and electronically sign results.

Users can send messages to patients about abnormal results and upload them to the patient portal when necessary. Users can compare the results using graphs.
