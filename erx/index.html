Seamless Solutions for Safer Prescribing and Lab Integration

Enhance patient care with our cutting-edge ePrescribing, PDMP, and eLabs features—streamlining workflows, ensuring compliance, and improving safety.

eRx, PDMP & eLabs

  1. Seamless ePrescribing & EPCS Integration: Effortlessly send electronic prescriptions (eRx) and manage controlled substances with EPCS.
  2. Refill Request Management: Electronically receive and respond to refill requests, streamlining pharmacy communication.
  3. Electronic Prescription Cancellation: Cancel prescriptions securely and electronically for instant system-wide updates.
  4. Efficient eRx Change Handling: Accept and respond to prescription change requests directly from pharmacies for streamlined adjustments.
  5. PDMP Integration for Safer Prescribing: Access real-time PDMP scores when prescribing controlled substances, supporting patient safety.
  1. Comprehensive Drug Formulary Checks: Instantly verify drug formularies to ensure cost-effective and covered medication options.
  2. Drug Interaction & Allergy Checks: Automatically check for drug-to-drug and drug-to-allergy interactions, enhancing prescription accuracy and safety.
  3. External Medication History Import: Seamlessly retrieve and import external medication history into the patient’s chart for a complete view of the patient.
  1. ONC Certification Compliance: Stay ahead with eRx and EPCS solutions that meet the latest certification requirements set by the ONC.
  1. Bi-Directional Lab Connections: Electronically connect with over 40 labs, including LabCorp and Quest, through a bi-directional interface for seamless order and result management.
  2. Real-Time Lab Result Alerts: Receive alerts when lab results are available, ensuring timely review and follow-up.
  3. Electronic Result Signing: Providers can review and electronically sign lab results for faster processing and compliance.
  4. Patient Communication: Notify patients about abnormal results and upload lab reports to the patient portal when necessary.
  5. Graphical Result Comparison: Compare lab results over time using intuitive graphs to track patient progress and trends.
