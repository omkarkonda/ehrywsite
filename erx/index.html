Seamless Solutions for Safer Prescribing and Lab Integration

Enhance patient care with our cutting-edge ePrescribing, PDMP, and eLabs features—streamlining workflows, ensuring compliance, and improving safety.

eRx, PDMP & eLabs

  1. Seamless ePrescribing & EPCS: Send electronic prescriptions and manage controlled substances securely with integrated EPCS functionality.

  2. Refill Request Management: Electronically receive and respond to refill requests, streamlining pharmacy communication.

  3. Prescription Cancellation: Cancel prescriptions electronically for instant updates across systems.

  4. eRx Change Handling: Accept and respond to pharmacy-initiated prescription change requests for quick adjustments.

  5. PDMP Integration: Access real-time PDMP scores to enhance client safety when prescribing controlled substances.

  1. Drug Formulary Checks: Instantly verify formularies to ensure medications are cost-effective and covered by insurance.

  2. Drug Interaction & Allergy Checks: Automatically check for drug-to-drug and drug-to-allergy interactions to improve prescription accuracy.

  3. Medication History Import: Seamlessly retrieve and integrate external medication history into the client’s chart for a comprehensive view.

  1. ONC Certification Compliance: Leverage eRx and EPCS tools compliant with the latest ONC certification requirements.

  1. Bi-Directional Lab Connectivity: Connect with over 40 labs, including LabCorp and Quest, for seamless order placement and result retrieval.

  2. Real-Time Lab Alerts: Receive notifications when lab results are available for timely review.

  3. Electronic Result Signing: Review and sign lab results electronically for faster processing and compliance.

  4. Client Communication: Notify clients of abnormal results and upload lab reports to the client portal as needed.

  5. Graphical Result Comparison: Compare lab results over time using intuitive graphs to track client progress and trends.
